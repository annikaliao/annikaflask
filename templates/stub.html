Who Am I?

Hi! I'm Annika Liao, a sophomore at Del Norte High School. I enjoy music, movies, art, and fashion.

Why APCSP?

I took this class because of my interest in technology! Especially after Covid, I noticed how prominent technology is in all of our daily lives. Thanks to this, I started experimenting with what I could do with technology. So far, I've only really experimented with the creative side, such as motion graphics, animation, AR, and art. I thought that taking APCSP would be a great way to ease myself into the more practical, coding side of technology. I was really nervous about this class before the start of school, and the first few weeks were stressful, but I'm glad I pushed through. I feel like this class has opened a whole world of opportunities for me, and I've met a lot of amazing people along the way.
